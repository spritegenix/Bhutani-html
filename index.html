Featured Highlights from M3M The Cullinan

High Design & Huge Customer Traffic

M3M The Cullinan is designed by DP Architects, the firm behind iconic structures like Dubai Mall and Singapore Sports Hub.
Located on the Noida Expressway, the area witnesses significant vehicular movement, with over 500,000 vehicles passing daily, making it a prime location for businesses.

Unlimited tenant options and business diversity

The development offers a mix of high-street retail shops, premium showrooms, food courts, and office spaces, catering to a wide range of businesses. This diversity ensures a steady flow of customers and reduces dependency on a single business type.


High Rental income and property management

M3M The Cullinan provides assured returns on investment, offering financial security to investors. Additionally, the project has initiated leasing activities, ensuring that commercial spaces are occupied by reputable tenants, thereby guaranteeing rental income.
